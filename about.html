About

  • Exercise Catalog was created by Malachi Barton as a Capstone Project. He is a senior Information Science student at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock.
  • Exercise catalog is hosted on Github Pages.
  • Our catalog uses ExerciseDB from RapidAPI.
  • Exercise Catalog was inspired by Malachi’s fitness hobby and his despise for blog-based fitness websites.
